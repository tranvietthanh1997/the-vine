We are so glad you are considering to visit The Vine. We are a community of people in Hong Kong who are passionate about God, one another, and the world around us.

We gather throughout the week in community settings and come together for worship on Sundays. Gatherings centre on the presence of God and the teaching of His word, with the purpose of empowering us as we partner with God in the reconciliation of all things.

However you connect with us, our desire is that you would discover a place where you can creatively explore and deepen your faith and journey in life with others.

GROWING BIG PEOPLE

Our vision at The Vine is simple – we want to grow big people. Through God’s presence, His Scriptures, and relationships with one another, we seek to help each person deepen their walk with God and become more like Jesus.
Like the early church, we desire to build each other up in love, using our gifts to serve God and one another, so that we would reflect Christ more fully in the spheres of influence He has placed us in.

CONNECT

Build authentic relationships that inspire your faith and provide support for life’s joys and challenges. There is a community for everyone here.

LEARN

Discover who you are in Christ, deepening your faith and becoming more like Him through courses that deal with a variety of different topics.

CARE

Find healing and comfort through prayer, counselling, or spiritual support. We are committed to walking with you through any season.

SERVE

Use your God-given gifts to bless, serve others, and grow in community. There are many opportunities, and we welcome you to get involved.
